Regular Meetings

Friday Night Program

Every Friday we gather as believers and seekers where we run various activities from praise and prayers, to game nights, bible studies, and many more! Our goal is to seek God in our weekly programs and grow in unity. We meet from 6:30PM-8:30PM in KTH B124, for more information e-mail hamilton.ccf@gmail.com!

Year Meetings

Each year meets weekly depending on the availability of other members. This time is designed for you to grow with other brothers and sisters in Christ in your individual years through praying with one another and other activities.

Second Year: Wednesday 7-9pm UH B126G
