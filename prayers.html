Prayer Request

Here is where as a fellowship we can keep each other accountable through prayer. As Christians we believe prayer is powerful as it’s our way to communicate with God.

Fellowship

  • Pray for the upcoming Frosh to come into McMaster! Starting university can be a scary transition. Pray that they find a community and they can feel God’s presence on campus.
  • Pray For the leaders as they have to plan for the upcoming year.

Around the Campus

  • Pray for the different fellowships that we will be able to spread God's love and his words around the campus more.

City of Hamilton/Toronto

  • Pray for the people in the city that might not know God yet.
  • Pray for the churches for their different ministries.

Around the World

  • Pray for the people on mission trips, that through God they can spread the Good News around the world.
  • Pray for the persecuted churches.
